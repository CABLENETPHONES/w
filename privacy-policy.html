Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2024



Introduction

Welcome to CableNetPhones.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.


Information Collection

We collect information when you visit our website, view our ads, or call for assistance with finding internet, phone, and cable providers. This includes data on how you interact with our site, which may be collected using cookies and similar technologies.


Use of Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services, respond to inquiries, and show relevant ads. We may share non-personal data with our advertising partners, including Google, to optimize ad performance.


Google Ads

We use Google Ads to display relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history. You can adjust your ad preferences through Google’s Ad Settings.


Opt Out

You can opt out of receiving promotional emails or marketing communications by simply emailing us at optout@cablenetphones.com. We will promptly remove you from our mailing list and ensure that you no longer receive such messages.


Calls

We may collect and store call data to help with customer inquiries and connect you with service providers. Calls may be recorded for quality assurance.


Your Choices

You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that this may affect site functionality.


Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@cablenetphones.com.
